    Biography

    Hello World! I'm Chima Chibuike, a passionate Web Developer based in Lagos, Nigeria. I started my journey back in 2023 with continuous learning of programming languages, I've honed my skills in programming and developed a strong foundation in communication, teamwork and curiosity. Now i love combining the worlds logic and creative designs to make eye-catching, accessible, and user-friendly websites with the latest trends in the tech industry.

    Technology has always lead the world, The move towards increasing inclusivity and diversity in the industry through representation is of importance to me.

    Being a web developer has opened my eyes into seeing the world's problems being solved with the help of Technology resolution.

    When i'm not coding, you can find me listening to music and exercising to ease my stress and relax my mind and brain.

    I'm excited to make the leap and continue refining my skills with the right company and mentorship.
